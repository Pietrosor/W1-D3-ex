Focaccia with Caramelized onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appertizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Featured in:

Fresh cranberry recipes that go beyond the sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500grams) all-purpose flower
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons istant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for nrushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scripeoff rind with a paring knifeif desired, thought is not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium size mixing bowl, or in a stan mixer woth a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour
